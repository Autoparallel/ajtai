Criterion:
- $d = 2^n$ for some $n$ (is a power of 2)
- $t~|~d$ ($t$ divides $d$)
- $q \equiv 1 + 2t~(\textrm{mod}~4t)$

Example satisfying all the criterion:
- $q = 17$
- $d = 16 = 2^4$
- $t = 8$

Then $$1+2\cdot 8 = 17~(\textrm{mod}~32)$$
